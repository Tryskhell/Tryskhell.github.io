About Me

Who I am

My name is Pierre Cogné and I am 19. I am a curious and passionate student, mostly interested by Game Design and 3D modelisation and animation. I like to code bits and pieces of video games in my free time in either Gamemaker or Unity and create 3D models to animate on 3DS Max or Blender, but I am also a GM for DnD 5e and Hero System 6e.

What I like

Naturally, I like video games, my favorites being turn-based strategy games, Beat em Up and management games. I am a fan of tabletop RPGs like DnD, Cyberpunk or Hero, but also more casual play-by-posts. While I am not extremely interested by music, I love new retro wave, rock and rap.

What I can do

I can use 3DS Max and Blender both to create and animate models. I have also the base knowledge with Photoshop, Illustrator and Krita. While I'm still learning Unity, I can use it to code little games, and I sometimes code with Gamemaker in my free time. I have a good experience with group work and can be creative.

Where I learnt

I am currently in my first year in a Licence for Infography and Interactive Medias, but I did a preparatory year in the same school.

Previously, I passed a Science BAC in Engineering Sciences in highschool. I attended a Computering and Digital Science class, as well as a Movie-making class. I also created and managed a Tabletop Roleplaying Club in highschool.

Where I worked

I participated to the 2018 Emotional Game Jam, as a game designer and 3D animator using Blender.

In April 2018 I also did a week-long internship in the signaletic company Alpha Signaletic. In February 2014 I did a two days long internship in the video game company Happy Blue Fish. In Mars 2014 I did a three days long internship in the animation studio Zagtoon.
